**Superhero Battle with Cubetto and Hot Wheels Cars**

**Objective:** To program Cubetto to navigate a course using Hot Wheels cars as obstacles, while avoiding them to reach the finish line.

**Materials:**
- Cubetto toy
- Command blocks (forward, turn left, turn right)
- Hot Wheels cars
- A large play mat or a flat surface
- Cubetto base and programming board
- Tokens or stickers to mark checkpoints

**Setup:**
1. Place the play mat on a flat surface.
2. Arrange Hot Wheels cars randomly on the mat to create obstacles.
3. Place Cubetto at the starting point.
4. Place a token or sticker at the finish line to mark the end of the course.

**Gameplay:**
1. **Introduction:** Explain to the children that Cubetto is a superhero on a mission to save the day. The Hot Wheels cars are villains trying to stop Cubetto. The children need to program Cubetto to navigate through the course and reach the finish line without crashing into the Hot Wheels cars.

2. **Instructions:**
   - Introduce the command blocks: forward, turn left, and turn right.
   - Explain that Cubetto will move forward when the forward block is placed, turn left when the turn left block is placed, and turn right when the turn right block is placed.
   - Demonstrate how to place the blocks on the programming board and how Cubetto will follow the instructions.

3. **Game Play:**
   - Divide the children into teams or pairs.
   - Each team takes turns programming Cubetto to navigate the course.
   - The team must place the command blocks on the programming board to create a path for Cubetto.
