**Knight's Rescue Mission**

**Objective:** Help the knight rescue the princess from the dragon's castle using Cubetto.

**Materials:**

* Cubetto programming toy
* Barbie dolls (princess and knight)
* A small obstacle course (e.g., blocks, cones, or small hurdles)

**Instructions:**

1. Set up the obstacle course with the dragon's castle at the end.
2. Place the princess Barbie doll at the starting point.
3. Give each child a Cubetto and a knight Barbie doll.
4. Explain that the knight must use Cubetto to navigate through the obstacle course and rescue the princess.
5. Introduce the three Cubetto command blocks: forward, turn left, and turn right.
6. Demonstrate how to program the knight to move through the course using the command blocks.
7. Challenge the children to program their knight to reach the princess using the fewest number of command blocks possible.
8. As the children program, encourage them to think critically about the best path to take and how to overcome obstacles.

**Variations:**

* Add a "dragon" obstacle that the knight must avoid.
* Introduce a "treasure" block that the knight must collect on the way to the princess.
* Have the children work in pairs to program the knight and princess to work together to overcome challenges.

This game encourages problem-solving, critical thinking, and programming skills while promoting creativity and imagination in preschool children.
