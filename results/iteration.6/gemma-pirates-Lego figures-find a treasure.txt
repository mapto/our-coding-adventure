##  Ahoy, Mateys! Treasure Hunt with Cubetto!

**Objective:** Guide your pirate Lego figure to the hidden treasure using Cubetto!

**Materials:**

* Cubetto robot
* Cubetto command blocks (forward, turn left, turn right)
* Lego pirate figures
* Lego bricks to build a simple obstacle course (optional)
* Treasure chest (can be a small box or container)

**Setup:**

1. Create a simple obstacle course using Lego bricks (optional). This can include walls, bridges, or other fun challenges.
2. Place the treasure chest somewhere in the course.
3. Each child gets a pirate Lego figure and a Cubetto robot.

**Gameplay:**

1. Explain to the children that they are pirates searching for buried treasure.
2. Show them how to use the Cubetto command blocks to move their robot forward, turn left, and turn right.
3. Encourage them to experiment with different combinations of commands to navigate their pirate figure through the obstacle course and find the treasure.
4. The first child to successfully guide their pirate to the treasure chest wins!

**Tips:**

* Start with a simple course and gradually increase the complexity.
* Encourage children to think about the sequence of commands needed to reach the treasure.
* Use visual aids like arrows or drawings to help children understand the direction of movement.
* Celebrate each child's success and encourage them to try again if they don't find the treasure immediately.


This game helps children develop their spatial reasoning, problem-solving skills, and understanding of basic programming concepts in a fun and engaging way.
