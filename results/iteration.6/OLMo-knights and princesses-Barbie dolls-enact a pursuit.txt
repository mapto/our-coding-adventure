**Game Title:** The Royal Chase Adventure

**Objective:** The knights must rescue the princess from the castle using Cubetto. The Barbie dolls narrate the story and guide the knights.

**Setup:**
- Place the castle model at one end of the room.
- Position the princess Barbie doll near the castle entrance.
- Scatter obstacles (pillow forts, tape lines) around the castle to create a maze.
- Position the knight Barbie dolls at the starting point, each with a Cubetto robot.

**Rules:**
1. **Storytelling:** The Barbie dolls start the story, explaining the knight's mission and the princess's location.
2. **Cubetto Commands:** Knights use three commands: forward, turn left, and turn right, to navigate through the maze.
3. **Obstacle Navigation:** Knights must navigate through the obstacles using the correct commands, guided by the Barbie dolls' instructions.
4. **Rescue:** The first knight to reach the princess and return to the starting point wins.

**Gameplay:**
- Barbie dolls act as narrators, providing clues and guiding the knights.
- Knights use Cubetto to move through the maze, solving puzzles by choosing the right path.
- Encourage teamwork: Knights can help each other by calling out commands or adjusting obstacles.

This game combines storytelling, physical activity, and problem-solving, making it engaging and educational for preschoolers.
