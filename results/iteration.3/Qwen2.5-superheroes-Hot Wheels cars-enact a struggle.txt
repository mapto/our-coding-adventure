**Superhero Adventure with Cubetto**

**Objective:** Help your favorite superhero save the day by racing Hot Wheels cars through a dangerous city!

**Materials Needed:**
- Cubetto toy
- Command blocks (forward, turn left, turn right)
- Hot Wheels cars
- A map or a simple city layout (you can draw one on paper or use a board game map)

**Scenario:**
You are a brave superhero, and your mission is to save the city from a villain who has taken over the streets! Your trusty Hot Wheels car is your vehicle, and Cubetto will help you navigate through the city.

**Instructions:**
1. **Set Up:**
   - Place Cubetto at the starting point of your city map.
   - Place your Hot Wheels car at the starting point.
   - Place the villain’s lair at the end of the map.

2. **Mission:**
   - Your mission is to drive your Hot Wheels car from the starting point to the villain’s lair.
   - Use Cubetto to move the car forward, turn left, or turn right.

3. **Commands:**
   - **Forward:** Move the car one step forward.
   - **Turn Left:** Turn the car to the left.
   - **Turn Right:** Turn the car to the right.

4. **Strategy:**
   - Plan your route by placing the command blocks in the correct order.
   - Remember, you can only use three command blocks!

5. **Example:**
