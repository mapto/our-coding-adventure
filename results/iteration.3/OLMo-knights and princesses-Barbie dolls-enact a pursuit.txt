**Title: The Royal Chase**

**Setting:**
In the enchanted kingdom of Araminta, a brave knight named Sir Lancelot and his trusty steed, Cubetto, are tasked with a crucial mission: to rescue Princess Isabella from the clutches of the wicked sorcerer, Malakar. The princess is held captive in a tower at the far end of the enchanted forest. To reach her, Sir Lancelot must navigate through a maze of trees, over a babbling brook, and past a sleeping dragon.

**Characters:**
- **Sir Lancelot:** The brave knight, controlled by the Cubetto robot.
- **Cubetto:** The knight's loyal steed, also the tangible programming toy.
- **Princess Isabella:** The damsel in distress, represented by a Barbie doll placed at the end of the maze.
- **Malakar:** The villain, not physically present but hinted at by obstacles in the maze.
- **The Dragon:** A sleeping obstacle, represented by a soft toy dragon placed in Sir Lancelot's path.
- **The Forest:** Made of green construction paper trees, creating a maze for Sir Lancelot to navigate.
- **The Babbling Brook:** A small, safe stream made of blue paper, representing a challenge to cross.

**Objective:**
Sir Lancelot must use his wits and Cubetto's commands to navigate through the forest, cross the brook, and reach Princess Isabella without waking the dragon.

**Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:**
   - Set up the forest maze on the floor using green construction paper trees.
   - Create the babbling brook with blue paper and safe materials to ensure it's harmless.
   - Place the sleeping dragon toy at a strategic point in the maze.
   - Position the Barbie doll (Princess Isabella) at the end of the maze.

2. **Starting Point:**
   - Begin the scenario at the entrance of the forest, where Sir Lancelot and Cubetto are ready for their quest.
