## The Knight's Quest

**Story:**

Once upon a time, there was a brave knight named Sir Cubetto. He loved his princess, Princess Barbie, very much. One day, a mischievous dragon stole Princess Barbie away! Sir Cubetto must use his courage and his trusty robot friend, Cubetto, to rescue her!

**Materials:**

* Cubetto robot
* Cubetto command blocks (forward, turn left, turn right)
* Barbie doll (Princess Barbie)
* Another doll or toy (the dragon)
* A playmat or large sheet of paper to represent the castle grounds

**Scenario:**

1. **Setting the Stage:**

   * Place Princess Barbie in a corner of the playmat, representing her captured tower.
   * Place the dragon doll somewhere else on the playmat, representing its lair.

2. **The Quest Begins:**

   * Explain to the children that Sir Cubetto needs to find Princess Barbie.
   * Have the children program Cubetto to move forward until it reaches the dragon's lair.

3. **Facing the Dragon:**

   * Once Cubetto reaches the dragon's lair, explain that the dragon is blocking the way.
   * Have the children program Cubetto to turn left and then forward to go around the dragon.

4. **Rescuing the Princess:**

   * Finally, have the children program Cubetto to move forward until it reaches Princess Barbie.
   * Celebrate Sir Cubetto's success in rescuing the princess!

**Extension Activities:**

* **Obstacle Course:** Create an obstacle course for Cubetto to navigate, using blocks, toys, or other objects.
* **Different Paths:** Explore different programming sequences to reach Princess Barbie.
* **Storytelling:** Encourage children to create their own stories about Sir Cubetto and Princess Barbie.


This scenario allows children to learn basic programming concepts while engaging in imaginative play. It also encourages problem-solving and spatial reasoning skills.
