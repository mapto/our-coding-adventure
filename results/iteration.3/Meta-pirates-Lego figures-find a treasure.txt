**Pirate's Treasure Hunt**

**Age Group:** Preschool children (3-5 years)

**Objective:** Help the pirate find the hidden treasure using Cubetto commands.

**Materials:**

* Cubetto programming toy
* Lego pirate figures (at least 2)
* Lego pirate ship
* Lego island with a small cave (treasure location)
* Small toy treasure (e.g., a small chest or a gold coin)

**Setup:**

1. Place the Lego island with the cave in the center of the play area.
2. Position the Lego pirate ship near the island.
3. Place the Lego pirate figures near the ship.
4. Hide the small toy treasure in the cave.

**Scenario:**

Captain Blackbeak and his trusty pirate friend, Barnaby, are on a mission to find the hidden treasure. They have a map that leads them to the island, but they need your help to navigate through the jungle using Cubetto.

**Instructions:**

1. Place the Cubetto in front of the pirate ship.
2. Explain to the children that they will use Cubetto to help Captain Blackbeak and Barnaby find the treasure.
3. Demonstrate how to use the Cubetto command blocks: forward, turn left, and turn right.
4. Ask the children to program Cubetto to help the pirates reach the island.
5. As the children program, ask them to describe what they are doing and why they are making those decisions.
6. Once the pirates reach the island, ask the children to program Cubetto to help them navigate through the jungle and find the treasure.
7. Encourage the children to experiment and try different paths to find the treasure.

**Challenge:**

* Can the children program Cubetto to help the pirates find the treasure in the fewest number of steps?
* Can they find a way to avoid obstacles in the jungle?

* Observe the children's problem-solving skills and ability to think critically.
* Evaluate their understanding of sequencing and programming concepts.
