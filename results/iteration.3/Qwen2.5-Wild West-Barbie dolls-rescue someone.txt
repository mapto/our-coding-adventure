**Wild West Rescue Adventure with Cubetto**

**Objective:** Help Cubetto rescue a Barbie doll from the Wild West!

**Materials Needed:**
- Cubetto toy
- Command blocks (forward, turn left, turn right)
- Map of the Wild West (printed or drawn)
- Barbie doll (or a small toy)
- Small obstacles (like rocks or cacti) to place on the map

**Scenario:**
Cubetto is a brave little robot who lives in the Wild West. One day, his friend, a Barbie doll, gets lost in the desert! Cubetto needs your help to find her and bring her back home.

**Instructions:**
1. **Set Up the Map:**
   - Place the map on the floor or a table.
   - Add some obstacles like rocks or cacti to make the path more challenging.
   - Place the Barbie doll somewhere on the map.

2. **Cubetto’s Mission:**
   - Cubetto needs to find the Barbie doll and bring her back home.
   - Use only three command blocks: forward, turn left, and turn right.

3. **Cubetto’s Journey:**
   - Start by placing the forward command block.
   - Then, place the turn left or turn right command block to navigate around obstacles.
   - Repeat these steps to move Cubetto closer to the Barbie doll.

4. **Rescue the Barbie Doll:**
   - Once Cubetto reaches the Barbie doll, use the forward command block to move her to a safe spot.
   - Celebrate with a cheer or a dance!

**Tips:**
- Encourage children to think about the path Cubetto needs to take.
- Discuss the importance of planning and problem-solving.
- Praise their efforts and creativity in solving the puzzle.
