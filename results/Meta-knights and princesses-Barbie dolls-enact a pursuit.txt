**Game Title: "The Brave Knight's Rescue"**

**Objective:** To create a fun and interactive storytelling experience using Cubetto, where preschool children can use their problem-solving skills to help a brave knight rescue a princess from a dragon's lair.

**Materials:**

* Cubetto programming toy
* Barbie dolls (princess and knight)
* A simple obstacle course or a designated play area
* A small toy dragon (optional)

**Game Description:**

In this exciting game, the children will take on the role of a brave knight who must rescue a princess from a dragon's lair. The princess will be represented by a Barbie doll, and the knight will also be a Barbie doll. The dragon's lair will be a designated play area or an obstacle course.

**Gameplay:**

1. The children will start by placing the princess Barbie doll in the dragon's lair, and the knight Barbie doll at the entrance of the lair.
2. The children will use the Cubetto programming toy to create a sequence of commands to help the knight navigate through the lair and reach the princess.
3. The three Cubetto command blocks that will be used are:
	* Forward: to move the knight one step closer to the princess
	* Turn left: to change direction and avoid obstacles
	* Turn right: to change direction and reach the princess
4. The children will need to use their problem-solving skills to create a sequence of commands that will help the knight reach the princess without getting stuck or running into obstacles.
5. As the children create their sequence of commands, they can use the Cubetto to test their solution and see if the knight can reach the princess.
6. If the knight successfully reaches the princess, the children can celebrate their success and try to create a new sequence of commands to make the knight's journey even more challenging.
