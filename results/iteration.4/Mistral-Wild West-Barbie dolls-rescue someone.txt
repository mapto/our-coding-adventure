Embark on an exciting Wild West adventure with Cubetto, the friendly wooden robot, and a group of imaginative preschoolers! The children's mission is to guide Cubetto through the town, navigating the saloon, the general store, and the sheriff's office, to rescue their Barbie friends. The game encourages problem-solving, spatial reasoning, and storytelling skills.

Using only three Cubetto command blocks, children can program Cubetto to move forward, turn left, and turn right. By combining these simple commands, they can create complex paths to navigate the town and overcome obstacles.

With each successful rescue, the children's confidence grows, and they can take on more challenging paths and obstacles. This engaging and educational game not only helps children develop essential programming skills but also fosters creativity, collaboration, and a love for learning.
